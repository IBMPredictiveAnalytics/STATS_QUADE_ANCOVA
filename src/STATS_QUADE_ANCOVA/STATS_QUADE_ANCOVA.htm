Quade's Nonparametric ANCOVA Test of Equality of Conditional Population Distributions

Gives Quade's (1967) nonparametric analysis of covariance test of equality of conditional population distributions. Also gives pairwise comparisons among all groups using the approach discussed in Huitema (1980). These are LSD uncorrected comparisons.


References

Huitema, B. (1980). The analysis of covariance and alternatives. New York: Wiley.

Quade, D. (1967). Rank analysis of covariance. Journal of the American Statistical Association, 62(320), 1187-1200.



Data Requirements and Operations:

The dependent variable and covariate(s) must be defined as Numeric. The grouping factor variable must be Numeric and not a Date or Time variable.

Cases are deleted if they are missing on any named variables (listwise deletion).

Split files and case selection are not allowed. Splits are turned off and all complete cases are used.

Weights are allowed, but fractional or non-integer values are truncated to integers.



Installed Menu Location:

This extension's dialog box is installed in the menus under Analyze>Nonparametric Tests.
